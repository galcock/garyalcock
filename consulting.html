Overview

I work with private equity firms, independent sponsors, and family offices on corporate carve-outs, platform builds, and special situations where sourcing, execution, and practical operating judgment matter as much as capital.

  • Corporate carve-outs: stand-up of divested divisions, multi-plant transitions, and stranded cost clean-up.
  • Special situations: distressed and complex M&A, covenant issues, and out-of-favor assets that require hands-on work.
  • Proprietary deal flow: targeted, mandate-specific outreach to generate off-market opportunities aligned with your themes.

Sectors

My transaction and operating work has spanned a range of sectors with a common thread: real businesses with moving parts, physical assets, and complex stakeholders.

  • Industrials and manufacturing — including metals, fabricated products, and contract manufacturing.
  • Distribution — route-based, technical, and value-added distributors.
  • Aerospace and defense (A&D) — components, services, and niche suppliers.
  • Media and technology — legacy and digital content, tech-enabled services, and niche software.
  • Healthcare — services and equipment with operational complexity.
  • Business services — facilities, field services, and recurring B2B offerings.
  • Consumer — including packaging, medical devices, food products, and beverages.

For more detail on current and prior mandates, see gtacompanies.com.

Engagement Models

Typical ways I work with clients:

  • Deal flow mandates: month-to-month retained sourcing focused on a narrow set of themes, with a clear expectation of proprietary opportunities rather than brokered deals.
  • Transaction support: targeted help on live deals, including carve-out planning, operating model build-out, and early-stage value creation plans.
  • Special projects: one-off projects where a sponsor wants a second set of eyes on a difficult asset, a portfolio problem, or a new strategy.
