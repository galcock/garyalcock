How I typically help

Proprietary deal sourcing

Building and running sourcing programs that surface 2–8 qualified proprietary opportunities per month in tightly defined themes — without relying on broad auction processes.

Focus: platforms and add-ons in industrials, manufacturing, and business services; careful alignment with each client’s underwriting discipline.

Carve-outs & complex situations

Corporate divestitures, messy separations, and situations where the heavy lift is operational and technical, not just financial.

Support ranges from targeting and thesis formation to exec-ready carve-out plans and integration roadmaps.

Independent sponsor support

Helping independent sponsors and small funds present institutional-quality materials, refine their themes, and avoid wasting cycles on low-probability situations.

Typical engagements: thematic build-outs, process design, and deal-by-deal feedback on fit and positioning.

Founder & CEO advisory

Working directly with founder-owners considering a sale, recap, or growth capital — with an emphasis on clarity, not pressure.

I’ve sat on both sides of the table and aim to make the process legible and deliberate for management teams.
