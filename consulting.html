Overview

I work with private equity sponsors, independent sponsors, and family-backed platforms on sourcing and executing transactions in industrials, manufacturing, distribution, and related sectors.

  • Proprietary deal flow for platform and add-on acquisitions.
  • Carve-outs, distressed assets, and other complex or time-sensitive situations.

  • Project-based support on carve-outs, distressed sales, and special committees.
