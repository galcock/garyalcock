Research — Density Field Dynamics

DFD is a scalar-field reformulation of gravity and optics in which a single field ψ on flat ℝ³ sets both the optical index and test-mass acceleration:

  • Optics: \( n = e^{\psi} \), so the local one-way speed of light is \( c_1 = c\,e^{-\psi} \).
  • Dynamics: test-mass acceleration \( a = (c^2/2)\nabla \psi \).

The framework reproduces standard post-Newtonian tests in the weak-field limit while predicting sector-resolved violations of Local Position Invariance (e.g. cavity–atom clock comparisons) and specific numerical relations between the MOND acceleration scale, α, and H₀.
