Research & Publications

My research focuses on scalar refractive descriptions of gravity and their implications for optical clocks, equivalence principle tests, and cosmological phenomenology. The current program is published under the umbrella Density Field Dynamics (DFD).

Topics

  • Scalar refractive field ψ and optical metrics
  • Optical clock comparisons and Local Position Invariance (LPI) tests
  • Acceleration scaling from galaxies to atomic systems
  • Laboratory falsifiers: cavity–atom ratios, matter-wave interferometry

Selected Preprints & Data

  • Density Field Dynamics: Unified Derivations, Sectoral Tests, and Experimental Roadmap — framework, field equations, and proposed tests.
  • Solar-Locked Differential in Ion–Neutral Optical Frequency Ratios — ROCIT data analysis with open methods and scripts.
  • Two Numerical Relations Linking the Fine-Structure Constant to Gravitational Phenomenology — parameter-free relations linking α to acceleration scales.

Preprints and code are hosted on densityfielddynamics.com and archived on Zenodo with open data packages. All documents should be treated as work in progress pending peer review.
