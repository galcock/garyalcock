Profile

Private equity, technology, and precision physics.

My background spans LBO and distressed investing, corporate divestitures, and operational value creation across industrials, manufacturing, tech, and media. In parallel I’m developing a scalar-field framework for gravity and light — Density Field Dynamics (DFD) — with lab-grade experimental predictions.

What I do

I split my time between three domains: capital allocation, experimental/theoretical physics, and building products. They feed each other: the finance work forces discipline; the physics forces clarity; the product work forces execution.

Private equity & special situations

I’ve worked across the full deal cycle — from proprietary sourcing and carve-outs to distressed and operational turnarounds — primarily in industrials, manufacturing, energy, and consumer products.

Past roles include Managing Director at Liberty Bay Capital and Revolution Capital Group, and advisory work at Houlihan Lokey. I’ve led or supported 50+ transactions with aggregate value over $10 billion.

Physics & Density Field Dynamics

I’m developing Density Field Dynamics (DFD), a scalar-field framework in which a refractive index field ψ governs both light propagation and test-mass acceleration, with explicit, lab-testable deviations from General Relativity.

Recent work includes a PPN analysis, strong-field and gravitational-wave sector, and optical clock LPI proposals in dialogue with leading clock groups.

Augmented reality & game systems

I build on Apple’s VisionOS stack — real-time scene reconstruction, shader-driven environments, and MMO-style AR gameplay — to explore how spatial computing changes how we work and play.

Current projects include a Vision Pro MMORPG that paints the real world with dungeon-style geometry and shared enemy encounters.

Advisory & board work

I occasionally advise funds, independent sponsors, and founder-owners on deal strategy, capital structure, and technology decisions that actually move value.

Typical mandates: deal sourcing architecture, carve-out strategy, and executive-ready materials for complex or cross-border transactions.

Optical clocks & LPI

Re-analysis of long-baseline optical clock comparisons (ROCIT 2022 and related datasets), with an emphasis on whether photons and atoms respond identically to the same energy-density environment and how to test that cleanly.

Focus areas: LPI-style anomalies, sector-resolved cavity–atom ratios, and height-separated experiments using existing clock infrastructure.

Carve-outs & industrial platforms

Corporate divestitures and buy-and-build theses in industrials, metals, and manufacturing, with a bias toward messy situations where operational work and tech upgrades create the actual value.

Experience spans metal fabrication, industrial distribution, packaging, and specialty manufacturing.

Writing & teaching

Long-form work on how private equity actually operates, how to think about risk as a practitioner, and how new physics ideas interface with real experiments.

Includes the in-progress book Skip the MBA, Own the Company and technical DFD manuscripts.
